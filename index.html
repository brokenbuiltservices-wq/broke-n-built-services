Transforming Spaces, Building Dreams Since Day One

At Broke N Built Services, we live by our motto — "We Brake the Old, We Build Extraordinary." We tear down the outdated and rebuild it into something remarkable. With over 5 years of industry experience and 50+ successful projects, we've earned a reputation for transforming ordinary spaces into stunning realities.

From residential makeovers to commercial fit-outs, our skilled team handles every aspect of renovation with precision, creativity, and passion. We specialize in electrical and plumbing, false ceilings, painting, flooring, partitions, custom interiors, and more. Every project we undertake is a reflection of our commitment to quality, timelines, and client satisfaction.
